about insect.christmas:


> in 2022, i started this website as a rejection
of modern social media websites
that present you with little control.

decades ago on the web, you were
allowed more control over your profiles.

many had their own websites, custom built.

i wanted to be able to display everything, anything,
and all of my work on my own website, ran & programmed by me, astrid ztar.

i am the keeper of my own domain.

i'm well aware that websites aren't exactly
the best advertising tools in the modern world,
but i love my website dearly.

design history:


december 12th, 2022
> humble beginnings...
> i'm not sure if this was the literal first design,
but it's certainly an early one.



march 31st, 2023
> over time, i filled up the home page with more content.
> this iteration actually removed all existing pages.
the entire website existed on one page: index.
> this is a fairly ugly & chaotic collage, but it has some charm.



december 19th, 2023
> about a year after launch, the website starts to resemble some
old y2k space jam-esque website or something.
> i got sick of how modern-looking this was.
> this was also the final design using a website builder,
before i jumped into coding everything from scratch.



february 20th, 2024
> in 2024, i decided to completely rehaul the website.
i abandoned my website builder, and hosted my own html, css & js code.



may 7th, 2024
> eventually, i felt like i needed a website directory.
this was the beginning of the true navigation bar.
there used to be a lot more pages in the navigation section.
also, my god. look at all that empty space...



august 22nd, 2024
> here i realized that the navigation bar could be placed on the left,
making for a much more compact & maximalist design.
> we also have the first design featuring the insect.christmas mascot, izzy the bug.
> the website also began to have its own original soundtrack,
with custom songs on each page.



september 26th, 2024
> i had always heard about web developers of old tinkering
with their web page made with tables.
so, i made a web page made from tables.
much more compact & sleek.



october 28th, 2024
> custom navigation bar icons, anyone?
> better mobile & small screen scaling? yes please!



january 27th, 2025
> more nav icons! randomized cat pictures!
> a cool butterfly that follows your mouse! wow!



may 1st, 2025
> new animated nav buttons! now with togglable rain fx!
> you can also toggle the butterfly cursor trail!
> more pages! more fun! more tables! yippee!
