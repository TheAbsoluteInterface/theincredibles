artistically, it can be very satisfying to limit yourself to a small set of tools. modernity brings forth many digital and technical inventions that increase productivity, but sometimes that can feel very overwhelming creatively. i have always found pleasure in stripping back layers and going back to the basics. instead of drawing on a computer with infinite undo's, dozens of layers, and unlimited colors, try returning to a piece of paper and a pencil. you'd be surprised how much creativity you can squeeze out of such few resources.
